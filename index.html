Our Advantages

SMART SMS offers Excellent SMS service on a state-of-the Art SMS platform, which already processed billions of messages and connected to 1000s of applications to faciltate an smooth flow critical SMS to the Public and getting the people Closer & Closer.

The platform is highly secure, scalable and extensible custom features. It provides businesses with a high quality interface to send and receive SMS messages with multilingual (English and Arabic) text with scheduling and long character support. SMART SMS messaging can be easily integrated through a rich set of API (Application Programming Interface) into the enterprise’s back-end infrastructure. SMART SMS is the ideal communication channel to stay connected with your customers, clients creating a real time network of relevant information.
